# GreenGuard

GreenGuard is a web platform that makes fighting deforestation simple, personal, and trackable. Users can sponsor trees that appear on a live map, businesses are ranked on sustainability impact, volunteers can join funded campaigns, and citizens can submit geotagged illegal logging reports. The platform also includes an eco merchandise concept where profits can support additional tree planting.

## Core Roles

- Individual sponsors fund tree planting.
- Business accounts fund impact and appear on the public leaderboard.
- Volunteers join local planting efforts.
- Citizen reporters submit illegal logging evidence.
- Admins verify and moderate reports.

## Main Features

- Interactive map showing deforestation zones, tree records, and reporting activity.
- Tree sponsoring records with species, quantity, and coordinates.
- Business sustainability leaderboard.
- Volunteer participation model for local planting teams.
- Citizen reporting tool with geotagged evidence.
- Eco store concept to support additional funding.

## Ethics (<=150 words)

GreenGuard is designed to protect both forests and people. Illegal logging reports and donations can be submitted anonymously, and user data is never sold to third parties. Reports are not publicly shown as verified incidents until moderation and authority escalation steps are completed, which reduces false accusations and protects reporters from retaliation. The eco store is intended to list only vetted sustainable suppliers to avoid greenwashing. We also publish a transparent monthly funding breakdown so users can see where money is spent and confirm that donations are creating measurable, intended impact.
